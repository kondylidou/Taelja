Axiom 1: p(Z)
Axiom 2: q(b,Z)
Axiom 3: q(X,Y) /\ p(Y) => q(f(X),Y)

Lemma 11: q(f(b),Z)
Proof:
  have q(b,Z)
    by axiom 2
   and p(Z)
    by axiom 1
  hence q(f(b),Z)
    by axiom 3

Goal 1: q(f(f(b)),a)
Proof:
  have q(f(b),a)
    by lemma 11
   and p(a)
    by axiom 1
  hence q(f(f(b)),a)
    by axiom 3
